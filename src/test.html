Corrosion Prevention

Corrosion is a natural process — a degradation of metal when oxygen comes in contact with MS or multi-metal surfaces. Preventing it at an early stage increases product life and reduces surface preparation and labour costs.

Every final equipment or machine requires time between 2 to 60 days. We manufacture various types of rust preventives — water-based, solvent-based, and oil-based — solving rusting issues during assembly, transportation, installation, storage, machining, and packing.

Our water-based corrosion-resistant materials offer rust prevention from 15 days to 20 years — the result of 25 years of research, teamwork, and over 2200 laboratory tests.

This is the most cost-effective and performance-based solution.

We offer a large variety of corrosion prevention solutions.

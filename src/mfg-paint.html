Coatings

We provide durable and reliable coating solutions for lasting protection and performance.

Creative Business Systems caters wide range of high-performance Protective coating, rust preventives which fulfil all requirements of wide range of industrial, offshore, petrochemical, fertilizer, sugar mill, pulp & paper, civil requirements. We offer perfect coating systems that includes epoxy, polyurethane, heat resistance, tank liners, surface tolerant and high solid coating. In creative house, you can get total anti corrosive systems, from surface preparation to final coat, under a single roof. We can supply wide range of rust preventive coating, from which the surface can be prevent at initial time, which saves a necessary surface preparation cost & one can do quality application work.

The most experienced staff of creative Business Systems are paint technologist, NACE (National Association of Corrosion Engineering) qualified, Corrosion technologist, experienced more then 25 years in specific anti-corrosive field. The company meet international standard ISO 9001:2015 with complete testing standards, every stage production testing, produce high quality coatings.

Every unit has it own anti-corrosive requirement, because of different climatic condition, various temperate, chemical reactions, production methods etc.., then it required specific coating systems, we can serve it, as a customised coating system against various conditions.

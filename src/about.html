About Us

About

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions with a focus on innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

The Creative Business System was established by Mr. Dhiren Desai in 1994. He is a NACE (National Association of Corrosion Engineering) degree holder. He started with trading of coating materials and anti-corrosive applications. While doing this, he came across many environmental conditions at various projects, which produce acid fumes, alkalis, solvent fumes and various types of gases in different temperatures. He found that while giving the proper system of coating to a particular customer site, it is not only important what is the condition at the site, but also what are the conditions at nearby plants, where a proper coating system should be applied.

According to him, to remain and progress in any business, a company has to take care of two assets. The first asset is the company itself, for which they are working. The second asset is Creative Business System’s own asset — its employees. The company takes care of employee progress by providing training through professionals. The training includes marketing personnel and technical applicators, who receive continuous updates through small courses that align them with current market conditions. The company invests in employee training, which helps Creative Business Systems deliver quality products to customers on time and complete jobs as scheduled.

By giving proper coating systems for your assets, Creative Business Systems increases the life of your structures three to four times. This means saving money that would otherwise be spent rebuilding the structure. The proper coating system reduces plant shutdowns, directly lowering production costs — another way to save money.

Mr. Desai used to study environmental conditions at and around the site. He found that special coating materials were needed to protect the assets of a particular company. Considering these requirements, he started manufacturing special coating materials and thus progressed from a trader to a manufacturer. He started manufacturing in 1998 .

Our Team Strength

It is extremely important to understand not only which coating materials are selected, but also how they are applied to the surface. Even the best coating will fail to protect if it is not applied correctly. At Creative Business Systems, we ensure every coating application is carried out with precision and care. Since 1994, we have been working with a team of over 60 highly trained applicators who possess extensive field experience. For large-scale industrial projects, our operational capacity allows us to deploy up to 180 skilled applicators to ensure quality and timely completion.

R & D and Testing Facilities

Our paint manufacturing facility and application activities are supported by a state-of-the-art R&D laboratory, application workshop, and online testing systems. The R&D lab is equipped with advanced apparatus and pilot production equipment such as acid and alkaline corrosion testing chambers, solvent testing chambers, DFT calibration meters, viscosity devices, surface profile gauges, portable spray booths, ovens, and instruments for testing raw materials and final product quality.

Becoming an expert in coating application and manufacturing does not happen overnight; it takes years of R&D and application excellence to produce systems effective in today’s global market.

Responding to industry needs for corrosion control and food-compatible coatings, we developed a solvent-free coating with all required features. This led to PROMASTIC–555, a unique refractory coating effective against acids, alkalines, solvents, and gaseous corrosion. We thank our scientists, technicians, and employees for their contribution to this success.
